About Me

Hello, my name is Stamesha Bello. I am a graduate student at Northeastern University and am currently a candidate for a Master's of Science in Computer Science. I received my undergraduate degree in Biology -- as opposed to Computer Science -- as I'd originally planned to become a doctor. However, as I gained more knowledge of and experience in the medical field, I began to have my doubts. Only when I served as a virtual medical scribe, did I finally realize what I'd truly desired. I found the app used to write medical notes -- what it was and how it could be improved -- to be of much more interest than the day-to-day activities of the providers I'd shadowed. The medical visits I'd witnessed grew to be somewhat monotonous, while the behind-the-scenes use of AI and other technologies were far more fascinating. Through the help of Northeastern's ALIGN program, I was able to make a necessary career shift, and I've never looked back since. I am currently seeking an internship that will push my limits and cement my place in CS.
